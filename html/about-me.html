About Me

Hello! I am passionate about living an eco-friendly lifestyle and reducing my environmental footprint.

Over the years, I have adopted various waste reduction practices such as minimizing plastic use, composting organic waste, and supporting sustainable brands.

Some of my favorite green hacks include using beeswax wraps instead of plastic wrap, making homemade cleaning products, and upcycling old clothes into reusable bags.
