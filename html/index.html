Zero-Waste Principles

The zero-waste lifestyle is based on five key principles:

  • Refuse: Say no to unnecessary items and packaging.
  • Reduce: Minimize the amount of waste you produce.
  • Reuse: Use items multiple times instead of disposing of them.
  • Recycle: Properly recycle materials to keep them out of landfills.
  • Compost: Turn organic waste into nutrient-rich soil.

Waste Statistics

On average, a person produces about 1.5 tons of garbage every year. This highlights the importance of adopting zero-waste habits to reduce environmental impact.
