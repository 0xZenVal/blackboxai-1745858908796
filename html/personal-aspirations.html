My Zero-Waste Goals

  • Switch to reusable containers and bags for shopping.
  • Reduce single-use plastics by avoiding disposable cutlery and straws.
  • Start composting food scraps to reduce organic waste.
  • Buy in bulk to minimize packaging waste.
  • Support local zero-waste stores and farmers markets.

Challenges and How I Plan to Overcome Them

Adopting a zero-waste lifestyle comes with challenges such as convenience, availability of zero-waste products, and social habits.

To overcome these, I plan to:

  • Plan my shopping trips to include zero-waste stores.
  • Educate friends and family about the benefits of zero waste.
  • Keep reusable items handy to avoid using disposables.
  • Be patient and persistent in building new habits.
